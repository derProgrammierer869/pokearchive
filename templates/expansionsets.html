Expansion Sets

The third generation of expansion sets was released in the summer of 2003, and was concluded in early 2007, before the Diamond and Pearl sets were introduced. During this generation, ex, Delta Species, and Gold Star cards were introduced which were exclusive to this generation. This list will only cover the English sets. Japanese sets might be listed at a later date.
